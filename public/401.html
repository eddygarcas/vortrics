Ups! Connection refused from external service

Vortrics wasn't able to reach the external service, either because a private secure connection is required or it requires extra login information.

Edit your credentials and enter login data to access the external service.

In case of a private secure connection required, we advice you to clone Vortics on your systems and run it from there.

If you are the application owner check the logs for more information.
  • If you want to cancel your account instead, edit your credentials and press 'Cancel my account'
